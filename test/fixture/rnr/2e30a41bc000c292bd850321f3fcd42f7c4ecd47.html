CLSTRFCK started out in 2012 with DJs BillyDoll and LanceDontDance wanting to create a dance party rich in PARTY JAMS.
Always trying to take it to a new level, you will find a mix of Hip-hop/rap, dance, indie, and the right amount of throwbacks and guilty pleasures. Also mixing in tracks ranging from moombah to trap, DJs BillyDoll and LanceDontDance strive for party feel. They are looking to party, and want you to join them. So get ready to drink, shake it, and break it.
