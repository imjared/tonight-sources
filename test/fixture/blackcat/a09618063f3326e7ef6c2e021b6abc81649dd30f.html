Friday June 13

Black Cat Summer Comedy Series with:

STEVE HOFSTETTER

MATTY LITVACK

$12 Mainstage / Doors at 9:00

What hasn't Steve Hofstetter done? The original columnist on CollegeHumor. The former host of Four Quotas and The Sports Minute (Or So). A comedy album that went to #1 on the iTunes charts. And appearances on everything from ABC's Barbara Walters Special to E's True Hollywood Story. There is a reason his shows are always packed. Come see why.

Get tickets from Ticketfly or from the Black Cat Box Office from 8p-12a (cash only).
Take Metro! Black Cat is located just three blocks from the U Street/Cardozo Metro Station.
